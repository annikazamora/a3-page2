Lunch and Dinner

We getting serious now

State & Main - 578 Weber St, Waterloo, ON, Canada

Grilled Chicken Club
Price: $20.50 CAD + tax

Had to ask for no avocado cause why was there avocado in there to begin with? Grilled chicken was yum, but I wish the lettuce wasn't shredding because it kept falling out. Instead, it should be one big leaf of lettuce. The fries on the other hand, definitely one of the best fries I've ever had. There was seasoning on it which I feel like is cheating because fries should just be potato and salt, but since it was too good I'll look the other direction.

Overall Rating: 8.9/10
Would I Repurchase?: Of course

Jacuzzi - 94 Kensington High St, London, United Kingdom

All'Arrabbiata
Price: £18

Hello! The pasta in Europe is just built different in general but this was so tasty. It didn't have too many spices and was nice and simple which allowed the taste of the ingredients to really show. Ate the whole thing and felt great. Would honestly move to Europe for the pasta alone.

Overall Rating: 14/10
Would I Repurchase?: Booking a flight there right now

Pizzeria Badiali - 181 Dovercourt Rd, Toronto, ON, Canada

Pizza Slice: Burrata Marinara, Margherita, Vodka
Price: $4.75-$6.25 CAD per slice + tax

Definitely the best pizza I have ever had. They had so many flavours to choose from I could literally eat the whole menu.

Overall Rating: 9.9/10
Would I Repurchase?: For sure

Pike Place Chowder - 1530 Post Alley, Seattle, United States

Seafood Bisque in a Bread Bowl
Price: $11.95 USD + tax

Never have I ever had soup from a bread bowl, but now I have. The soup was quite good but if it were served in a regular bowl it wouldn't be anything special. I love bread so the fact that it is holding my food already increases the rating. Was the bread a bit stale? Yes. But since it is in bowl form I can ignore that.

Overall Rating: 7.8/10
Would I Repurchase?: I'd repurchase the bowl but maybe try a different soup flavour

Via della Palombella, 37, 00186 Roma RM, Italy

Spaghetti Al Pomodoro Fresco e Basilico
Price: €10

Pretty sure this restaurant was targetted for tourists so I guess it worked cause I ended up choosing this restaurant to eat dinner at with my family. The ingredients are so simple, but because it's so fresh, it tastes so good and not bland. I still think about this pasta I can't lie.

Overall Rating: 10/10
Would I Repurchase?: Please yes
